Identity without Identification.

The sovereign verification layer. Distinguishing biological entities from synthetic agents via economic consensus.

The Manifesto

In a network flooded by synthetic intelligence, trust requires proof of biology. However, you shouldn't have to trade your face, your iris, or your government ID to prove you are human.

0xHuman verifies the act of humanity, not the actor. We validate your existence through trust and physical entropy, not biometric surveillance.

Core Mechanics

1. THE HUMAN CHAIN

An Uplink (validator) and Downlink (user) structure tied by economic bonds. A self-policing network of trust.

2. SOVEREIGN VERIFICATION

Method agnostic. Whether it’s a video call, a physical letter, or a meet-up, the proof is decided between peers.

3. ANTI-ABUSE LOGIC

"Right to Exit". Users can reject an assigned Uplink and request a re-roll without penalty.
